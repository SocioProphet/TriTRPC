# Algorithm Transition Plan

- Maintain explicit suite negotiation (`research`, `fips-classical`, `cnsa2-ready`).
- Keep research-only algorithms out of standards-inspired and approved-like deployments.
- Prefer TLS 1.3 semantics where available.
- Preserve cryptographic agility by negotiating suites independently from frame encoding.
- Review the algorithm inventory quarterly and whenever NIST transition guidance changes.
